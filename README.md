Note: Currently works with the PUBLIC BETA branch only

Adds a new playable character to the game, based on the cult British sci-fi sitcom Red Dwarf. Featuring a unique mini card set for each of the four main characters, Lister, Rimmer, Cat and Kryten, with new card mechanics based on their personality traits and classic quotes & events from the show.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

Smeg: Disgust enemies to reduce their attack damage by half
Confidence: Works like strength but is lost when damage is taken
Paranoia: Reduces confidence
Sustenance: Stack 10 food to gain 2 energy
Radiation: Area damage that halves each turn
Scrap: Loot scrap and use cards to forge into damage, block, energy, gold etc.
Despair: Insult enemies to increase damage by the stack%
Justice Field: Reflect damage back at enemies

....Plus lots of other unique card effects to discover!
